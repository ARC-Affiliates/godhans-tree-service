What Time of Year Is the Cheapest for Tree Removal?

Late fall through early spring (November through March) is the cheapest time for tree removal in Jacksonville, with prices typically 15-30% lower than peak season rates due to reduced demand and optimal working conditions. Tree service companies experience their slowest period after hurricane season ends and before spring growth begins, creating opportunities for cost-conscious property owners. The dormant season also provides better visibility of tree structure without foliage, allowing arborists to work more efficiently and safely.
